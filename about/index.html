About

BSides Kraków is the biggest Drupal community event in Texas.

Come learn about every aspect of Drupal with sessions presented by you—members of the Drupal community.

Program

Friday, June 21

  • Full-day workshops
  • Web Leadership Day - Free!
    Join the thought leaders of Austin's tech community in this day of curated talks about the future of the web.
  • Geek Trivia Night

Saturday, June 22

  • Keynote: Shawn O'Keefe
  • Sessions
  • Half-day workshops
  • BoFs*
  • Official after party

Sunday, June 23

  • Keynote: Danese Cooper
  • Sessions
  • BoFs*

* BoFs are "Birds of a Feather" sessions. We will provide a dedicated space for people to hold ad-hoc discussions and presentations about topics or areas of interest.

Venue

Geek Trivia Night

On Friday night at 7pm, join us at Capital Factory (701 Brazos St) and test your geek cred and win fabulous prizes at our very first Trivia Night.

Official after party

After the first day of sessions on Saturday, we'll be kicking it at Buffalo Billiards for the official after party. Come relax and socialize with great food, drinks, and music in the heart of Austin's famous Sixth Street.
